Bacon Letticue Tomato & Avacado

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices beefsteak tomato
  • 1/3 Cup Bread and butter pickle brine
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 6 slices thick-cut bacon, cut into 6-inch strips
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 TBSP lemmon juice
  • 1/2 TSP Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 4 slices Pullman bread, toasted
  • 4 Bibb Letticue leaves, washed

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 Degrees F.
  2. Place tomato slices in baking dish. Pour pickle brine over tomatoes and season with pepper to taste.
  3. Place in the refigerator for 30 mins up to 2 hours.
  4. Arrange the bacon in a single layer on a rack set inside a sheet tray. Season with 1/2 TSP black pepper.
  5. Roast the bacon until crispy, 10-12 mins per side.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine mayo, lemon juice, worcestershire and garlic. Mix until well blended. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  7. Season the avocado with salt and pepper.
  8. Assemble the sandiches by spreading the aioli on each slice of bread.
  9. Layer 2 slices of bread with letticue and then avacado. Top the other 2 slices of bread with bacon and tomatoes (let the brine drip off first).
  10. Cut the sandwiches in half serve and enjoy!!!
