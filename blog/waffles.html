Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 4 TSP Baking Powder
  • 2 TBSP White Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 Cups Warm Milk
  • 1/3 Cup Butter (Melted)
  • 1 TSP Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder & sugar
  3. Prehead waffle iron to desired temperature
  4. Separate bowl, beat eggs. Stir in Milk, Butter and Vanilla
  5. Pour the milk mixture into the flour. Beat until it is blended
  6. Laddle the batter into preheated waffle iron.
  7. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.
  8. Serve and Enjoy!!!
